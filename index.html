About Baraka Cyber

Welcome to Baraka Cyber, your one-stop destination for all digital and document services in Gilgil, Nakuru County, Kenya. Established in 2015, we've been serving the local community with state-of-the-art technology and expert assistance.

Located in the heart of Gilgil town, we're easily accessible to residents, students, and visitors alike. Our prime location near Gilgil Sub-County Hospital and the Gilgil National Youth Service Training College makes us a convenient choice for a wide range of customers.

Our Mission

At CyberHub, our mission is to bridge the digital divide by providing affordable, high-quality cyber services to the Gilgil community. We aim to empower individuals and businesses with the tools and support they need to thrive in the digital age.
